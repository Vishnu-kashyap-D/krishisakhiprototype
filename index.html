Empowering Farmers with Technology

Our platform bridges the gap between complex agricultural data and practical farming decisions, making precision agriculture accessible to everyone.

The Challenge

Farmers and policymakers lack easy access to localized, real-time data on groundwater and soil health, leading to inefficient water usage and suboptimal crop yields.

Our Solution

Krishi Sakhi provides a unified platform that uses AI to analyze complex datasets from sources like INGRES. Our virtual assistant makes this data accessible through simple queries, interactive maps, and personalized alerts.
